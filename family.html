Get to know about my Family!

As you saw on the main page I grew up the Philippines. I have't seen my biological father since the day I was born, nearly 17 years. I am the only daughter of my mom, who raised, guide, and protected me, alone. But I have a lot of cousins from my mother side who is like a siblings to me too. Growing up in the Philippines, my world was small, I only have my mama and grandpa but sadly we lost him last year. My mama is my entire foundation —the one who worked tireless hours to make my life better, making sure our little home felt full even when the chairs around the table were mostly empty. I still remember how many dogs I had back then. They were my comfort zone because I was alone most of the time.
