Hello I'm from the Philippines!

The family is central to Philippine society. They maintain close relationships, and multigenerational living arrangements are common. Respect for authority is valued and honored. Sometimes, family members work abroad to support the family, called Overseas Filipino Workers (OFWs).

Pet Dogs

Dogs are domesticated members of the family. Dogs have been bred for desired behaviors, sensory capabilities, and physical attributes. Over 340 dog breeds that vary widely in shape, size, and color. Compared to humans, dogs possess a superior sense of smell and hearing. Dogs are valued for companionship, loyalty, and their ability to be trained for various tasks. They require regular exercise, a balanced diet, proper veterinary care, and socialization to thrive.

Benefits of Ownership

Owning a dog reduces stress and loneliness, increases social interaction, and encourages a more active lifestyle, often boosting owners' happiness

About Dogs

Facts About Dogs

  • Dogs are the most popular pet on planet.
  • Dog sweat through their paw pads, not their skin.
  • Dogs are able to breathe out through their mouth and nose at the same time.
  • They can learn over 100 words and gestures.
  • Dogs use body language to express their feelings.

Dogs Behavioral Issues

  • Aggression: Fear-related, food-related, territorial, and leash-related.
  • Separation Anxiety: Destructive behavior, howling, pacing, and house soiling when left alone.
  • Destructive Behaviors: Chewing furniture, digging, or scratching.
  • Unruly Behavior: Jumping on people, leash pulling, and stealing items.
  • Inappropriate Elimination: Urinating or defecating in the house.
